This year's event was marked by an even more extravagant display than previous years, showcasing watches intricately adorned with rare gemstones like diamonds, emeralds, and sapphires—a sight so grand that it evoked memories of Gatsby-era splendor.

As the night drew to a close, whispers circulated among attendees about what they had glimpsed within those walls: A future where these timeless luxuries continue not just as decorative pieces but also as investments and keepsakes that are sure to be passed down through generations.
