The monstrous hurricane was initially categorized at Category 3 but intensified rapidly before making landfall on the U.S. coastline yesterday evening, catching many unaware and unprepared for such severe weather conditions in these regions which usually experience more mild temperatures year-round due to their geographical location south of major cities like Atlanta or Savannah.
