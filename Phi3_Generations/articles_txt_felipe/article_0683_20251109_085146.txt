Topic: Sports - Baseball

Baseball Hall of Fame candidate hits career-best home run as Major League rookies face off at Milwaukee's American Family Field on Tuesday night; local fans and sports experts are noticing a potential new star in the making, Johnathan Hale. In his first game back since recovering from an arm injury that had sidelined him for nearly two seasons, Hale delivered a spectacled performance with three hits including one memorable solo home run off Chicago Cubs pitcher Derek Jeter during their MLB All-Star exhibition match on July 18.

Johnathan's comeback has caught the attention of Milwaukee fans and baseball analysts alike, as he managed to hit a career high with his homemaker against one of Major League Baseball’s most revered pitchers at home under the lights. Hale took advantage of an opening in Jeter's defense by using a fastball on a low outside count followed up quick arm action towards the plate that caught everyone off guard, including himself as he said: "I felt like I had one shot to make this happen."
