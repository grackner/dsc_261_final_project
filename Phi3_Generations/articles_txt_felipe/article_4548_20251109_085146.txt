A sense of excitement, pride and an undercurrent of competitive spirit pervaded the atmosphere as supporters filled stands donning red or blue regalia with team insignia proudly displayed. The Georgia Bulldogs’ offense is renowned for their ground game while Alabama Crimson Tide thrives on high-octane passing assaults, a fact that made Saturday night's contest even more intriguing to fans and experts alike.
