This strategy by her company is regarded as a move towards consolidating power within Silicon Valley’s booming digital entertainment sector, raising concerns about market competition being unfairly tilted against smaller newcomers who rely heavily on venture capital funding for their initial growth stages to compete with the mogul's vast financial resources.

Meanwhile, industry experts are calling this media mogul an unsettling symbol of the concentration of wealth and influence at high levels within San Francisco's tech scene—raising questions about how new startups can compete with established companies backed by significant financial resources in Silicon Valley’s ever-evolving digital entertainment landscape.
