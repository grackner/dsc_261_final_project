Topic: Health - Mindfulness

A new mindfulness initiative launched in Los Angeles on Tuesday aims to help reduce stress levels among residents amidst growing concerns over mental health awareness and the impact of COVID-1 endless news cycle . The program, sponsored by the LA County Department of Health Services, will provide free workshops across 25 neighborhoods starting next month. "Mindfulness has proven effective in improving overall wellbeing," said Dr. Maria Gomez, chief psychologist for public health programs at the county's department during a press conference held yesterday. According to recent studies, mindful practices can reduce symptoms of anxiety and depression by up to 60%. The initiative comes after local surveys found that nearly one in three adults report high levels of stress within their daily lives. Workshops will cover techniques like guided meditation, breathing exercises, body scans, and yoga under the guidance of licensed therapists trained specifically for mindfulness practice delivery . The Department has already begun scheduling sessions in areas with higher populations such as Koreatown , Downtown LA, Eagle Rock, Silver Lake , Beverly Hills, Venice Beach among others. "We believe our diverse offerings can cater to the unique needs of every resident," Dr Gomez explained during her speech at city hall . Some 20 workshops have already been booked and are open for sign-up via an online registration portal available on the department's website www dot lahealth services. Next steps in promoting this new program are currently being planned, including collaboration efforts between local schools and community centers. The LA County Department of Health Services is hopeful that the positive feedback received during an initial trial run at select libraries last weekend can help shape how they move forward with expanding these workshops to other parts of Los Angeles county . "We are thrilled about this opportunity," commented one participant, Ana Martinez , who attended a session in Hollywood. “The techniques I learned helped me manage stress better and understand the importance of mental health,” she said. With mindfulness being embraced as an essential part to personal wellbeing nationwide, initiatives like these are becoming increasingly important for local communities.
