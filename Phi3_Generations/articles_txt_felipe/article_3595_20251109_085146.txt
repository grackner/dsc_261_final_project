One bar owner, John Smith shared his frustration with city officials during Tuesday evening: "We run a popular establishment here on this very street - St. George Square which has hosted thousands upon thousands of guests over decades celebrating our national history; yet today we sit empty waiting anxiously for progress reports amidst charred debris as though all past memories were turned to ash by flames themselves."
