A massive earthquake measuring a magnitude of 7.8 struck off the coast of Ecuador on Sunday evening. Residents in nearby cities felt intense shaking and numerous reports have surfaced regarding significant damage to infrastructure as well as casualties, with at least ten people confirmed dead so far. The quake also generated tsunami warnings for several countries across South America including Colombia, Panama, Honduras, El Salvador, Guatemala, Nicaragua and Costa Rica after seismic activity was detected by monitoring stations in the Pacific Ocean 250 miles from Ecuador's coastline.
