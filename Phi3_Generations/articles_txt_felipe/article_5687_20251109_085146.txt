The breakthrough was made possible through several years' worth of dedicated research in Brisbane, with international collaboration from leading labs. The batteries are solid-state and nonflammable—a significant safety improvement over traditional liquid electrolyte technology that has been prone to fire risks when damaged or left unattended for long periods.
