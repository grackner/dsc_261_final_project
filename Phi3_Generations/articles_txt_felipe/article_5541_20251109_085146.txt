The surge in violence comes amid reports released by Chicago's Metropolitan Police Department earlier this month indicating an overall uptick of 15% over last year concerning robbery-related aggravated assault incidents, with a disturbing increase reported specifically among theft occurrences during night hours between dusk till dawn.
