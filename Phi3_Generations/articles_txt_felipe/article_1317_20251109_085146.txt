CNN's methodology, which combines traditional survey sampling techniques with big data analytics, allows for a nuanced understanding of the electorate. "Our poll indicates increasing concerns over economic policies and foreign affairs under President Doe's term, pushing many undecided voters towards Smith," Brumley stated in an interview last weekend.

Even though Smith leads among undecided voters nationwide based on data from last Thursday survey and historical trends dating back to 2016 when Hillary Clinton lost her bid for presidency by a margin of less than two points, election experts are urging caution.
