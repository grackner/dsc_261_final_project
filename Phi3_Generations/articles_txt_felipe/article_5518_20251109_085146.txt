Everton FC's Newcomer Takes Early Fan Vote in UK for Best Local Players Annual Poll with Record-Breaking Numbers: 'It was a dream come true to be recognized like this.' - Marcus Williams, Liverpool Echo Reporter. Everton has made history by leading the count at halftime of Britain's largest basketball fan poll conducted online and via mail across 30 cities nationwide in February as part of UKBallers Annual Best Local Players Poll for first-year player Marcus Williams from Cheshire, who was nominated alongside seasoned players. As early as noon Tuesday when the vote count began to close before hitting its record numbers at full stop around 3 pm with over a quarter million votes cast in total and Everton' end up leading by double digits according to UKBallers President Jane Smith. 'We are incredibly proud of Marcus who has impressed everyone not just on court but also for his leadership skills, academics excellence along the way,' said Ms. Smith while pointing out that Williams will receive a cash prize from Everton FC' charity fund after securing this honour at such an early stage in their career alongside many experienced veterans. 'This achievement by Marcus is well-deserved given his undeniable talent and dedication to the sport,' added Liverpool Echo reporter Marcus Williams who spoke about it, sharing what he said was a dream come true for him being recognized like this at such an early stage in their career alongside many experienced veterans. 'I'm grateful and honoured by all of you here today', concluded Smith further adding that there will be special celebrations planned soon as the counting comes to its final stages with details announced later during a community gathering organized locally, which also features several players invited for an open talk including Marcus Williams himself who expressed his gratitude towards the supporters and friends saying it was all 'unbelievable.' - Emma Johnson, Sports Reporter.
