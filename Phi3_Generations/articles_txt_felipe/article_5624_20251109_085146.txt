"Luxurious exploration lies at the heart of our city, which is why we see such grand investments in initiatives like these," stated Sheikh Rashid Mohammed Al Maktoum during his unveiling speech last Thursday evening where he expressed confidence that this venture will not only boost Dubai's tourism but also invigorate the luxury fashion industry.

The gallery, once completed and officially opened in January 2025, is expected to feature an eclectic mix of international high-end designers who have been selected based on their commitment towards sustainable practices alongside aesthetic appeal; ensuring that style doesn't come at the expense of our planet. The curated collection boasts pieces with CADMOS (Costume and Design Materials Optimized for Sustainability) certification, which indicates strict adherence to eco-friendly processes in designing and manufacturing luxury fashion items; a testament that style can be both extravagant and conscientious.

"This is not just another art gallery or mall - it's an experience where visitors will feel like they are stepping into the world of haute couture, all while being mindful about our environment," said renowned fashion designer Amal Hassan Al-Marhoon in a press conference. As head curator for this groundbreaking project, she has already started working closely with leading brands to finalize selections that will be displayed across five themed galleries within the building - each designed by top architects who specialize in sustainable designs and materials reflecting Dubai's distinctive landscape.

This unique approach not only captivates fashion lovers, but also appeals to those looking for an immersive art experience that pushes beyond just aestheticism; merging style with eco-consciousness in one fell swoop - truly embodying Dubai's reputation as the 'City of Gold.' The opening night celebrity guests list includes leading fashion influencers, celebrities and dignitaries from around the globe.
