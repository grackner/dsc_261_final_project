Today's full approval will be especially important as cases of pediatric COVID continue to rise; with over 5,000 hospitalizations among children aged zero through fourteen since December last year according to the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC), an increase that coincides with waning vaccine protection.

Experts say this news will help parents feel more confident in their decision-making as they navigate these difficult times; offering a sense of relief while continuously seeking updated recommendations from healthcare providers, including pediatricians and infectious disease specialists to keep children safe during COVID's ongoing impact.
