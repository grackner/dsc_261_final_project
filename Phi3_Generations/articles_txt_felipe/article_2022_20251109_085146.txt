The penthouse is located just 10 blocks away from other landmarks within Beverly Hills including Rodeo Drive, The Getty Villa Museum at Laurel Canyon Parkway intersection as well as various iconic restaurants and boutiques where guests can enjoy their social engagements post-event before heading back home for another grand evening or continuing onward to explore additional luxury homes dotting the prestigious Los Angeles landscape.
