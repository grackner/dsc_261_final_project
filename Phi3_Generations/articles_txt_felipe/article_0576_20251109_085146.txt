CNN News: Stock Market Tumbles in Volatile Start to Week; Investors Urged Caution Amid Global Concerns

(New York) - Early today, the Dow Jones Industrial Average plummeted by over 500 points amid mounting fear of a recession and unstable political climates worldwide. On Thursday morning, U.S.-based stock indices opened significantly lower with a staggering decline of over 300 points witnessed by many on Wall Street traders alone - an ominous signal to the market and financial world as concerns about rising interest rates coupled with recent layoffs in major industries take hold, prompting investors' caution. "I was just looking at my portfolio when I noticed that it had plummeted by more than 20% since mid-February," said Sarah Thompson, a seasoned stock trader from New York City who has been closely following the market movement for years but now finds herself anxious and uncertain.
