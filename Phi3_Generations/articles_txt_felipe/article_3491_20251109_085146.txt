In an unprecedented event that blends art with social commentary and political activism, a large group of artists gathered at New York's Metropolitan Museum on April 15th to create what has been dubbed "The Statue." This massive piece stands in stark contrast against the sleek corporate-sponsored exhibits typically seen within museum walls. The event took place just days before International Women's Day, symbolizing a call for gender equality and women empowerment throughout history depicted through artistic representation across ages, cultures, and contexts from around the world.
