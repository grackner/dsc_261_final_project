The Cleveland Cavaliers and Boston Celtics are preparing for their latest clash tonight at Quicken Loans Arena in downtown Cleveland — an event that is sure to captivate both local fans and NBA enthusiasts around the country alike since these two storied franchises have met on numerous occasions throughout history.

Cleveland's Cavaliers, led by veteran head coach John Calipari, are looking to snap a four-game losing streak against their perennial rival Boston Celtics in this highly anticipated matchup tonight at Quicken Loans Arena downtown Cleveland from 7:30pm.

"We've come together as a team and worked hard over these past weeks leading up to the game," said Cavaliers Head Coach John Calipari during an interview earlier today, referring to his current roster of players who have shown resilience throughout tough losses against top teams this season. "Our focus is solely on basketball tonight and we're ready for whatever Boston throws at us."
