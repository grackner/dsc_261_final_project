East Coast Yoga Retreats Expand With New Wellness Program Focused on Mindful Living Amid Rising Stress Levels

By Olivia Smith  
August 15th, 2023   
CNN NEWS NETWORK    

New York – East Coast Yoga Retreats have announced a new initiative to address the growing stress levels among Americans. The retreat center in Boulder will launch an intensive Mindful Living program starting September 1st, aiming at teaching participants how to achieve mental clarity and emotional resilience through mindfulness techniques.

An estimated one-third of adults struggle with daily stresses that affect both their physical health and overall wellbein g,' reports Dr. Jane Wilson from the Boulder Wellness Institute, who will be part of this program alongside several professional yoga instructors and a licensed psychologist specializing in stress management techniques.

"We are seeing an urgent need to help people find peace within themselves amidst our fast-paced society," says Dr. Wilson. "This Mindful Living initiative will offer proven practices that empower individuals, not only on days spent at the retreat but also as a way of life after their return."

The program includes yoga sessions led by certified instructors Emily Johnson and Michael Rodriguez; guided meditation facilitated by renowned mindfulness expert Dr. Aaron Lee; cognitive-behavioral strategies to handle negative thought patterns, taught by psychologist Laura Hayes with the aid of evidence-based materials from top mental health texts; workshops on gratitude and kindness in relationships led by relationship coach John Davis; as well as mindful eating practices facilitated through a partnership with local organic farmers.

Participants will have access to serene settings within the retreat center, such as rooftop gardens or forest walks where they can engage in nature therapy sessions led by expert guide Maya Thompson and environmental specialist Dr. Sophia Chen who emphasizes a connection with nature's impact on mental health benefits.'

In addition to these offerings at the retreat, East Coast Yoga Retreats plans educational seminars for locals within Boulder City Hall every first Monday of each month starting October 5th and extending invitations worldwide through virtual sessions. The program will also host a series of webinars with experts discussing mindful living on Tuesday evenings at seven p.m., which are open to the public for free registration via their website until November ends.'

Participants echoed positive feedback about how quickly they felt transformed by these holistic approaches towards well-being – saying that 'living more intentionally has helped me feel happier and less overwhelmed,' said newbie yogi Rebecca Carter from New York. As for longtime retreat guests, the program is their first experience with an intensive Mindful Living course; they reported being astonished by how quickly these practices became part of daily life after just two weeks.'

Dr. Wilson expressed hope that this initiative would continue to grow nationwide and globally as more people seek healthier ways to manage stress in a modern world,' concluding with, 'Through education on mindfulness techniques at the retreat center coupled with our virtual resources reaching communities around the globe - we are creating opportunities for lasting change.'
