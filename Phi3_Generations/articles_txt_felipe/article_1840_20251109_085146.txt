“I have never seen anything remotely close to it in my 30 years here.” With Biles now sitting just a few spots away from taking home Olympic gold, experts believe that this performance might very well be remembered for generations as one of history’s great sporting moments—and perhaps more than her skill alone on uneven bars. “I can only imagine how many times she had to fall before mastering such an unbelievable stunt,” said a fellow American gymnast after witnessing the routine firsthand during practice earlier this week at NEC Park in Seoul, South Korea where Biles has been training alongside her teammates. “But then again, that's what makes Simone so special; even when she fails, it just motivates everyone to work harder."
