In closing remarks to his teammates and supporters last night after practice at Eastville High's gym: "I want every single one of you here tonight thinking about just how far we’ve come together this season," said Coach Thompson, a reminder that no matter the outcome on March 15th or any other game going forward, Eagles remain united in their love for basketball and each other.
