The storm is causing extensive damage, including downed trees and power lines throughout parts of Nebraska's capital city of Lincoln; Iowa City was not spared either with reports coming in overnight about damaged properties there as well [NWS images].

The system has already claimed one life - an elderly woman who lost her footing while walking on a sidewalk and fell victim to flying debris during Sunday's nighttime hours. It remains unclear if any other fatalities or injuries have been reported since then, but local officials are urging residents in the most affected areas not to venture out unless absolutely necessary [official statement].

"We understand this is a very challenging time for our communities and we encourage everyone's safety by avoiding unnecessary travel," said Mayor Greg Wagner of Lincoln. "Our crews work around-the-clock on storm response efforts, clear debris from roadways, restore power to areas where possible."
