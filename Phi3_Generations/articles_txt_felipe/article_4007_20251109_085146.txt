Carter's collection focused on repurposed fabrics sourced ethically from small-scale artisans within the UK, setting a new standard for environmentally aware apparel in haute couture circles. Her pieces were noted not just for their beauty but also reflected her journey as an advocate for sustainability and responsible consumerism in fashion.
