We look forward to working together towards creating a stronger competitive force in the tech industry.”

Market experts and financial analysts are closely watching how this unprecedented move will play out next, as well as potential regulatory implications that may arise from such an aggressive consolidation of power within Silicon Valley. With companies like ABC Tech Corp announcing their expansion moves during the day, trading volumes have risen significantly – a trend set to continue tomorrow and into the next week with this after-hour merger news still fresh on investors' minds.
