In an unprecedented cyberattack on major US technology firms today in Silicon Valley, hundreds of companies have been temporarily shut down as hackers exploit vulnerabilities to steal sensitive data and disrupt operations, reports CNN Business Tech News Service. The attack targeted over fifty tech giants, including Apple Inc., Google LLC, and Facebook Inc., leading experts believe the operation is part of a broader cyber espionage campaign by an unknown foreign entity aiming at intellectual property breaches starting yesterday evening through this morning's digital skirmishes.

As we stand witness to what can only be described as corporate warfare, security analyst James Miller from CyberSafe Consultancy remarked in light of the recent surge in such events: "It seems that our adversaries have no qualms about exploiting commercial enterprises for their own gains. The audacity and boldness behind this attack should be a wake-up call."

Affected organizations reported significant outages, with some unable to access critical services until restoration efforts are complete tomorrow morning. Facebook's official statement highlighted: "We continue our investigation into the matter while enscuring security measures and user privacy is not breached during this process," adding a note of transparency amidst uncertainty for users potentially impacted by stolen data, despite reassurances that no confidential information was compromised.

The FBI has been briefed on the situation and assesses it as highly sophisticated in nature while still under investigation to identify perpetrators behind this brazen act of cyber warfare targeting America's leading technology firms, with a timeline for comprehensive findings expected within weeks.

As industry experts warn businesses worldwide about the growing prevalence and complexity of these attacks — calling it not just disruptive but potentially transformative on global commerce dynamics in tech — this incident has prompted increased discourse amongst governments, companies alike as to how we can fortify our defenses against such threats. The full extent of damage yet remains unclear with a significant financial impact anticipated for the targeted sectors and possible cascading effects on global markets potentially escalating in coming days should this attack continue or similar ones arise, urging immediate reassessment of cybersecurity policies within an evolving digital landscape.
