Topic: Business - Pre-markets

As companies scrambled to provide commentary on the developments and analyst predictions grew darker in tone for some firms like Alphabet Inc., whose shares fell 5% after CEO Sundar Pichai's reassurances did little to quell fear. Amidst mounting concern among business leaders calling for immediate action on both sides as regulators weigh in but withhold official commentary until concrete evidence is gathered, industry watchdogs emphasize caution and recommend diversification during times like this while experts are urged not to make drastic changes without more information being made available.
