Smith recounted how everyone in their group felt during this unexpected celebration, saying that it brought them closer together as artists.

This extraordinary moment came to light when a journalist from CNN attended the film festival on Saturday night at Times Square's iconic Theatre 82 and witnessed firsthand what seemed an unusually strong bond forming between actors and audience members amidst their powerful performances of recent independent films showcased during the event. The three stars were seen taking each other's hand mid-performance, a sign that suggested more than just camaraderie but genuine connection after sharing compelling storytelling on stage before an incredibly responsive crowd.
