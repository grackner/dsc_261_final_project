The buzz around the stadium was that this set the tone of a highly contested and physical game ahead with both teams pushing their limits in search of victory.

In reaction to Manchester United's defeat, Chelsea manager Guus Hiddink praised his team saying, "Today we demonstrated our ability not only on paper but also on field showing why we are here." While the Blues celebrated their win and continued winning streak at 5-2 when they took a three-goal lead against Manchester United.
