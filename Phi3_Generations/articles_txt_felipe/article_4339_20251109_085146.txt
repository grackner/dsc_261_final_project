"In light of these developments and emerging trends, we believe that expanding educational resources on nutrition-related risks is imperative," concluded Dr. Richardson before concluding her interview with CNN's team aboard a food safety conference in Las Vegas this weekend aimed at fostering dialogue between industry leaders and government agencies over proposed changes to the FDA regulations that could reshape America's approach to specialty culinary products for decades.
