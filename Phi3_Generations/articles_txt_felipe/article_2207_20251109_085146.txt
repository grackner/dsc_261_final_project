His performance on September 5th not only elevated him within the team hierarchy but also put Boston squarely back into playoff contention ahead of their remaining series against rival New York, who are currently neck and neck with them in first place as they head to Yankee Stadium next Sunday for a rematch that will surely draw another crowd.
