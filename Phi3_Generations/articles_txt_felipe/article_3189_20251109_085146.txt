Topic: Health - Mindfulness

"In an effort to improve mental well-being and reduce workplace stress among its employees nationwide, a leading pharmaceutical company has recently launched a mindfulness program called 'MindCare.' The initiative started last month as the first phase of corporate social responsibility (CSR) in health.


As part of this ambitious project, MindCare offers workshops and online courses designed to teach employees various stress-reduction techniques such as meditation, yoga, deep breathing exercises, and cognitive behavioral strategies aimed at enhancing mental resilience. The program also incorporates regular 'mindfulness breaks' during the workday for personal reflection or relaxation time in company offices located across New York City to California since its launch on April 1st this year.
